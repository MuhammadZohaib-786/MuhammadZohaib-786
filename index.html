"The Islamic Republic of Pakistan"

Explore the Beauty
of Pakistan's Best Destinations

Discover breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and unforgettable adventures in Pakistan's most stunning locations. Experience the majestic peaks of the Himalayas, the serene beauty of crystal-clear lakes, and the vibrant colors of bustling local markets. From exploring ancient forts and palaces to trekking through lush valleys and pristine wilderness, every corner of Pakistan offers a unique journey. Whether you seek tranquil retreats or thrilling adventures, our diverse destinations promise memories that will last a lifetime.

Kashmir

Azad Kashmir: Azad Kashmir is an administrative region of Pakistan situated in the northern part of the country. The northern part of Azad Jammu and Kashmir encompasses the lower part of the Himalayas, including Jamgarh Peak 15,531 feet [4,734 meters].


Gilgit-Baltistan

Gilgit Baltistan: Gilgit-Baltistan is an administrative unit of Pakistan situated in the Northern Part of the country. It is one of the best tourist spots in Pakistan. Gilgit-Baltistan is home to five of the "eight-thousanders" and to more than fifty peaks above 7,000 meters (23,000 ft). Gilgit and Skardu are the two main hubs for expeditions to those mountains.

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Located in the northwestern region of the country along the international border with Afghanistan.

Punjab

Punjab: Punjab is Pakistan's second largest province by area, and it is the most populated province, with rich culture & history.

Balochistan

Balochistan: It is the largest province in terms of land area, forming the southwestern region of the country, but it is the least populated. Its provincial capital and largest city is Quetta.

Sindh

Sindh: Sindh is in the southeast of the country, and historical home of the Sindhi people. Sindh has Pakistan's second largest economy, while its provincial capital, Karachi, is Pakistan's largest city and financial hub.

Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, is ranked as one of the most beautiful cities in the world.
